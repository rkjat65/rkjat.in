About This Project

Comprehensive data analysis of all candidates in the 2024 Lok Sabha elections, examining party dynamics, criminal cases, education levels, and wealth declarations.

Key Findings

  • 43% of candidates have declared criminal cases
  • Candidates with criminal cases have 35% higher average assets
  • 4,500+ independent candidates contested
  • 65% candidates are graduates or above
  • Asset declarations range from thousands to hundreds of crores

Tech Stack

Data: Python, Selenium, Pandas, NumPy
Visualization: Plotly
Dashboard: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Source: MyNeta (ADR)
